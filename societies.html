The Quintessential Green Ribbon of Ceylon©

The Quintessential Green Ribbon of Ceylon is a premier environmental organization focused on reforestation efforts in Sri Lanka. Committed to reviving the island's lush greenery, the organization combines traditional ecological knowledge with modern sustainable practices to enhance forest cover and support local wildlife habitats.

The Ceres Fauna Foundation©

The Ceres Fauna Foundation is dedicated to the preservation and expansion of global green spaces through innovative reforestation initiatives. By partnering with local communities and leveraging cutting-edge ecological research, the foundation aims to restore biodiversity and combat climate change one tree at a time.

The Relief For Eksara Foundation©

The Relief For Asadisa Foundation is a humanitarian and environmental organization striving to mitigate the effects of deforestation in vulnerable regions.
