The Destruction of Sri Lankan Forests

Sri Lanka is a paradise of biodiversity. Known as the gem of the Indian Ocean, this small island is home to several different climates, environments and unique fauna. Over the past 100 years Sri Lanka has gone through many changes and one of these changes is its forests. The most serious environmental issue in Sri Lanka right now, is deforestation.

Deforestation is not an easy thing to conqure. There are many services that need the land and resources obtained by deforestation to function. Reforestation Efforts

The best way to reduce the damage done by deforestation is to restore the flora and fauna we destroyed! This process is what's known as reforestation. The least we can do as inhabitants of this planet is give back to the environment that surrounds and protects us!
